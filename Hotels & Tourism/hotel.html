Hotel

A hotel is a commercial establishment that provides paid lodging, usually on a short-term basis. It offers a range of accommodation types and services, from basic rooms to luxury suites, often including amenities such as restaurants, room service, housekeeping, and recreational facilities. Hotels cater to various travelers, including tourists, business professionals, and families, with options spanning from budget-friendly to high-end luxury experiences.

Hotel VERSION

UNIQUE Hotel MODEL IN SRI LANKA

CEC’s prime properties across Sri Lanka present a unique portfolio for exclusive villa and hotel developments that blend luxury, nature, and authentic experiences. "Pasikudah Eco Villa" concept—a serene, nature-immersive retreat perfect for eco-conscious travelers and wild elephant watching. Along the sun-kissed west coast, the Nattandiya plot near Marawila Beach is ideal for the "Golden Coconut Villa" and "Marawila Beach Hotel," where guests can enjoy coastal breezes, coconut groves, and easy beach access. In the cultural heartland of Narammala,could be transformed into a tranquil countryside escape, while in Colombo, prime urban locations provide the perfect backdrop for sophisticated stays like "Mango Shadow Pool Villa" and "Araliya Gardens Villa"—offering privacy, elegance, and curated urban greenery. Together, these sites enable CEC to create a diverse collection of hospitality experiences, from beachfront luxury to wilderness eco-retreats, all rooted in sustainability and scenic beauty.
