Anuradhapura Eco-Luxury Resort

The proposed development is a premium eco-luxury hotel and cabana resort project in the culturally rich region of Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka. The project blends modern tropical architecture with cultural sensitivity and sustainability.

It includes a four-storey hotel, luxury cabanas, infinity pool, wellness areas, and landscaped gardens designed for immersive guest experience.

Nearby attractions include Sacred City of Anuradhapura, Sri Maha Bodhiya, Ruwanwelisaya, Wilpattu National Park, and more.

The project is developed by Consulting Engineers and Contractors (Pvt) Ltd (CEC), focusing on sustainable tourism and economic growth.
