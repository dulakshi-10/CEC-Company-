Agromart

Agromart represents a digital transformation in Sri Lanka's agriculture sector, making farming more profitable, sustainable, and connected through technology.

AgroMart VERSION

UNIQUE AGROMART MODEL IN SRI LANKA

CEC Agromart represents a perfect fusion of modern retail and traditional farming. We are a growing enterprise that delivers vegetables and fruits cultivated in our own farms directly to our supermarket shelves. This unique farm-to-store model allows us to guarantee freshness, control quality at every stage, and offer our customers produce they can trust at competitive prices. Our shelves are stocked with a diverse selection of carefully handpicked items, chosen to meet the varied needs and tastes of our community. At our core, we believe that providing fresh, affordable, and high-quality food is fundamental to nurturing healthy families, which is why customer satisfaction remains the true measure of our success.

Our & properties

CEC Agromart Nawala

CEC Agromart is a unique supermarket chain specializing in fresh produce. Our key differentiator is our farm-to-table model—we grow our own fruits and vegetables, which allows us to ensure high quality and freshness while keeping costs affordable. We offer a wide, carefully selected range of produce to meet diverse customer needs. Every item is rigorously inspected by our experts, as our primary goal is complete customer satisfaction.

CEC Agromart Kynsey Road

CEC Agromart Kynsey Road outlet is dedicated to offering the freshest and finest vegetables and fruits directly sourced from our own farmlands across the island all at the most affordable prices in the city. Enjoy the authentic taste of nature along with a delightful range of freshly baked bakery items and premium coffee from Coffee by CEC, served daily at reasonable prices.
