Farms in Sri Lanka are diverse, focusing heavily on staple rice (paddy) and export crops like tea, rubber, coconut, fruits, and vegetables, forming a backbone of the economy and rural livelihoods, blending traditional organic methods with modern tech like solar pumps and polytunnels, supported by government policies and projects for sustainability and growth.

Farms VERSION

UNIQUE FARMS MODEL IN SRI LANKA

CEC’s agricultural properties in Weikanda (500 acres), Narammala (80 acres), and Nattandiya (10 acres) stand out as exceptional locations rich in natural beauty and sustainable farming potential. The vast Weikanda estate, bordered by forest reserves, is not only ideal for large-scale agriculture but is also famous for wild elephant watching, offering a rare eco-tourism experience. The Narammala property maintains traditional Sri Lankan farming combined with modern techniques, ensuring healthy, high-yield crops year-round. The Nattandiya farm, located near Marawila Beach, benefits from coastal breezes and fertile soil, making it perfect for coconut cultivation and scenic agri-tourism appeal. Together, these lands represent CEC’s commitment to sustainable agriculture, biodiversity, and environmental stewardship.

Our & properties

Weikanda 500 Acres

The Weikanda land spans a vast 500 acres and is known for its rich agricultural activities and natural beauty. The area supports a wide range of local agricultural practices, including paddy cultivation, spice crops, and other traditional Sri Lankan plantations. Due to its location close to forest reserves, Weikanda is also popular for wild elephant watching, offering visitors a unique opportunity to observe elephants in their natural habitat safely. The serene environment, fertile soil, and biodiversity make this property one of the most valuable agricultural and eco-tourism locations in the region.

Nattandiya Farm Land 10 Acres

Located close to the beautiful Marawila Beach, the 10-acre Nattandiya farmland sits amidst lush agricultural surroundings and widespread coconut plantations. The coastal breeze and fertile soil provide perfect conditions for coconut cultivation as well as various other local crops. This farm is uniquely positioned between agricultural landscapes and coastal beauty, making it not only a productive agricultural site but also an appealing location for visitors due to its scenic environment.

Narammala 80 Acres

The 80-acre property in Narammala is dedicated to local agricultural development and traditional farming. This land supports a variety of crops that thrive in the region’s favorable soil and climate conditions. Narammala is known for its peaceful rural environment, making it ideal for sustainable and high-quality agricultural production. The farm maintains traditional farming methods while also integrating modern techniques to ensure healthy, high-yield crops throughout the year.
