About Company

UNIQUE CONSTRUCTION MODEL IN SRI LANKA

CONSULTING ENGINEERS & CONTRACTORS (PVT) LTD, commonly known as CEC, is a major construction company in Sri Lanka, with CS-2 CIDA classification as a major road contractor for over a decade. Over the last 22 years CEC has grown from being a small independent contractor to a major construction and road development company, and is now recognized as a dependable contractor with a very high rating for delivering projects with great quality and on-time.
