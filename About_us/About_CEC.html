About Us

Consulting Engineers & Co (CEC), now rebranded as the CEC Snapd Companies, is a trusted leader in construction and civil engineering in Sri Lanka, with over 30 years of experience delivering quality projects. With strategic diversification in tourism, agriculture, security services, engineering, property development, and apartments, the CEC Group reflects our commitment to innovation and expanding horizons. Elevated to C1 CIDA grading and recently attaining 150 certifications, we continue to set benchmarks in excellence, driving development across industries and turning vision into reality.


Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted and diversified conglomerate, excelling in construction, engineering, hospitality, retail, and property development by delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-quality solutions that enhance lives and create lasting value for our communities and stakeholders.

Our Mission

To provide world-class services and products across construction, architecture, hospitality, retail, property development, and security, with a strong commitment to excellence, innovation, and integrity. We strive to exceed customer expectations, drive sustainable growth, and empower local industries.

CEC Company is a leading construction and engineering service provider in Sri Lanka. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, delivering high-quality solutions with a commitment to safety and innovation.
