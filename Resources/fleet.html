To ensure an unhindered supply, we have acquired sprawling quarry complexes across Sri Lanka with vast granite reserves. The bulk of our regular stone requirements is sourced from these quarries.

We continuously upgrade our quarrying facilities to harness the full potential of this valuable asset. By adopting cutting-edge techniques, we streamline mining operations efficiently.

Advanced machinery, equipment, and modern technologies are deployed across the quarry sites to maintain operations in line with international standards. We take pride in meeting demands of any scale for crushed products within committed timelines.

With strong infrastructure, abundant resources, skilled manpower, and modern transit provisions, we are fully equipped to conduct large-scale quarrying and fulfill both immediate and bulk processed product demands.

CEC Company is a leading construction and engineering service provider in Sri Lanka. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, delivering high-quality solutions with a commitment to safety and innovation.
